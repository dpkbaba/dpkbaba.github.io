Privacy Policy for School Vahan

 

 Effective Date: 20/06/23

 

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which School Vahan collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (hereinafter referred to as "User" or "Users") of the School Vahan mobile application (the "App"). This privacy policy applies to the App and all products and services offered by School Vahan.

 

1. Information We Collect

 

1.1 Personal Information:

School Vahan collects personal information from Users only if they have enrolled to use our services. This includes but is not limited to the following:

 

- Name of the User

- Contact information (such as email address and mobile number)

 

1.2 Usage Information:

School Vahan may collect non-personal identification information about Users whenever they interact with the App. This may include information about the device used to access the App, such as the device's unique identifier, operating system, and browser type.

 

2. How We Use Collected Information

 

2.1 Personal Information:

The personal information provided by Users is used solely for the purpose of providing and improving the services offered by School Vahan. This information may be used to:

 

- Authenticate the User's access to the App

- Provide real-time updates on the location of the school bus to parents and school authorities

- Send notification alerts regarding bus location and expected time of arrival to pickup/drop points

- Enable users to add identifiers for each bus, such as their own name or child's name

- Provide quick support through call functionality to address and resolve any issues

 

2.2 Usage Information:

Non-personal identification information may be collected and used by School Vahan to analyze trends, administer the App, improve user experience, and gather demographic information. This information helps us understand how Users interact with the App and allows us to make enhancements accordingly.

 

3. Data Security

 

School Vahan takes appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of personal information and usage information stored on our servers.

 

4. Sharing of Personal Information

 

School Vahan does not sell, trade, or rent Users' personal identification information to third parties. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding our Users with our business partners, trusted affiliates, and advertisers for the purposes outlined above.
